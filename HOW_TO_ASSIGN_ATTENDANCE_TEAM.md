# How to Assign a Team to Attendance

## For Super Admin / Admin

### Step 1: Go to Team Functions Settings
Navigate to:
```
http://localhost:8000/admin/dashboard/settings/team-functions
```

### Step 2: Select Your Chapter
If you're a super admin, select the chapter from the dropdown at the top.

### Step 3: Enable Attendance for a Team
1. You will see a list of all teams in that chapter
2. Find the **"Attendance"** toggle/checkbox
3. **Check the box** next to "Attendance" for the team you want to assign
4. Click **"Save"** or the changes will auto-save

### Step 4: Verify
The team is now assigned to handle Attendance. Team leads of that team will now see:
- **Attendance** menu in their sidebar
- Access to:
  - **Manage Attendance** - Create attendance sessions
  - **Check-in** - Mark attendance (Present/Absent/Late) with manual time entry
  - **Reports** - View attendance analytics and graphs
  - **Team Members** - View all members and create new members
  - **Subunits** - Create and manage subunits (Team Lead only feature)

---

## For Team Leads

Once your team is assigned to Attendance:

1. **Login** to your team lead dashboard
2. Look for the **"Attendance"** menu in the sidebar
3. Click to expand and access:
   - **Manage Attendance** - Create sessions for services/events
   - **Check-in** - Mark member attendance with time field
   - **Reports** - View analytics
   - **Team Members** - View and add new members
   - **Subunits** - Manage subunits

---

## What Each Page Does

### Manage Attendance
- Create attendance sessions
- Select: Service, Event, or Custom
- Choose date
- Close/reopen sessions

### Check-in
- Select an open session
- Search members by name
- Filter by team
- Click "Select" on a member
- Enter time (optional - manual entry)
- Click **Present**, **Absent**, or **Late**

### Reports
- Overall attendance percentage
- Absentee percentage
- Team attendance rates
- Member attendance rates
- Charts and graphs
- Filter by: 7 days, 1 month, 6 months, 1 year

### Team Members
- View all members in your chapter
- Search by name
- Filter by team
- **"+ Add New Member"** button to create new members
- Assign new members to teams

### Subunits (Team Lead Only)
- Create subunits within your team
- Assign subunit leaders
- Add members to subunits

---

## Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the Attendance menu"
- Make sure you're logged in as a Team Lead
- Make sure your team has been assigned to Attendance in settings
- Refresh the page

### "ACCESS DENIED" error
- Your team hasn't been assigned to Attendance yet
- Contact your Super Admin to assign your team

### "No open sessions available"
- Go to **Manage Attendance** first
- Create a new session for today's service/event
- Then go to **Check-in** to mark attendance
